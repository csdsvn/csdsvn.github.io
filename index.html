ABOUT US

Who We Are

The Center for Sustainable Development Studies (CSDS) is a Vietnamese non-governmental organisation focusing on youth empowerment, community development and international volunteer exchange. Founded in 2009 and based in Hanoi, Vietnam, CSDS develops and implements programs in different areas of the country to contribute to the sustainable development of Vietnam. Our vision is a society where young people are valued and empowered to act as innovative contributors to a sustainable and equal world. Our mission is to empower and support youth by providing them the skills and opportunities they need in order to become agents of sustainable change and development in their communities.
